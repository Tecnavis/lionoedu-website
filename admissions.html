College Requirements

At LionoEdu, we believe that creativity knows no boundaries. Our fashion designing courses are open to everyone, regardless of age or educational background. Whether you're a young enthusiast or someone exploring a new passion later in life, our programs are designed to bring out your best creative potential. With a focus on hands-on learning and industry-relevant skills, our courses cater to all levels, from beginners to those looking to refine their craft. Join us at LionoEdu, where passion, creativity, and innovation come together to shape the future of fashion, one designer at a time.

Transferees

At LionoEdu, we welcome transferees with open arms, ensuring a smooth transition into our fashion designing programs. Whether you’re switching from another institute or shifting focus within the fashion field, our courses are flexible to accommodate your unique background. No matter your previous educational journey, we’re committed to integrating your experience while providing the tools and knowledge necessary for growth. Our expert faculty ensures transferees catch up with ease and thrive in our creative environment. Join us at LionoEdu, where your past experience meets fresh opportunities, and together we craft a path to your future in fashion.

Stay connected with LionoEdu for updates, events, and news in the world of fashion design.
